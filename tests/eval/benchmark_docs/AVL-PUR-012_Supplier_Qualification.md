## 1. Supplier Classification

| Class | Definition | Qualification Requirement | Review Frequency |
|-------|-----------|--------------------------|-----------------|
| Strategic | Single or sole source, critical to operations | Full financial, safety, and ESG audit + board-level approval | 12 months |
| Preferred | Multiple qualified sources, non-critical | Standard qualification (financial stability, quality record) | 24 months |
| Approved | Commodity suppliers, easily replaceable | Basic qualification (business license, insurance) | 36 months |
| Provisional | New suppliers undergoing trial | Full qualification required within 90 days of first order | Ongoing during trial |

## 3. Supplier Performance Metrics

Key performance indicators for all Strategic and Preferred suppliers:
- On-time delivery rate (target: >95%)
- Quality acceptance rate (target: >99%)
- Lead time adherence (target: within ±5 days of quoted lead time)
- Incident response time (target: <4 hours for critical supply disruptions)
