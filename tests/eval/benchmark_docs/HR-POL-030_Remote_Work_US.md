## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes remote work eligibility, approval procedures, equipment standards, expense reimbursement, and stipend provisions for employees of AeroVelo Logistics US, LLC operating within the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

## 2. Remote Work Eligibility

### 2.1 Qualifying Roles

The following job families are eligible for remote or hybrid remote work arrangements:

- Logistics Planning and Scheduling (remote-compatible)
- Data Analytics and Business Intelligence (fully remote)
- IT Systems Administration (hybrid minimum: 2 days/week on-site)
- Finance and Accounting (hybrid minimum: 2 days/week on-site)
- Legal and Compliance (hybrid minimum: 1 day/week on-site)
- Executive Management (discretionary, per VP approval)

### 2.2 Non-Qualifying Roles

The following roles are explicitly excluded from remote work eligibility due to operational or safety requirements:

- Hub floor operations and warehouse personnel
- Field logistics coordinators (on-site freight handling)
- Equipment maintenance technicians (on-site)
- Security and access control personnel

## 3. Remote Work Stipend Program

### 3.1 Stipend Amounts

Eligible remote workers receive a monthly remote work stipend as follows:

| Arrangement Type | Monthly Stipend (USD) | Annual Maximum (USD) |
|-----------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Full Remote (5 days/week from home office) | $350 | $4,200 |
| Hybrid Remote (3 days remote per week) | $200 | $2,400 |
| Hybrid Remote (2 days remote per week) | $125 | $1,500 |

### 3.2 Equipment Provision

AVL provides a standard home office kit valued at $1,200 (one-time):
- 16" laptop (company-owned, loaned for duration of remote work)
- Monitor (27", company-owned, loaned for duration)
- Ergonomic chair (employee-purchased, reimbursed up to $600)
- Peripheral kit (keyboard, mouse, headset)

### 3.3 High-Intensity Remote Worker Surcharge

Employees classified as "High-Intensity" (defined as those performing 24/7 on-call incident response or crisis management duties) receive an additional $150/month surcharge, bringing the full remote stipend to **$500/month** ($6,000 annually).

### 3.4 Internet and Phone Reimbursement

- Internet: Actual costs, up to $80/month (receipts required)
- Business phone line: Full coverage provided at $45/month (no receipt required; auto-enrolled)

### 3.5 Total Maximum Annual Stipend (US Entity)

Under normal circumstances, the maximum total annual remote work benefit per employee is **$5,320** ($4,200 base + $1,800 internet reimbursement, excluding the one-time equipment provision of $1,200 and the on-call surcharge of $150/month if applicable).

## 4. Approval and Renewal

### 4.1 Initial Approval

Remote work requests must be submitted via the AVL HR portal (Form HR-030-RW). Approvals are granted by the employee's direct supervisor with Regional VP notification for arrangements exceeding 3 days/week remote.

### 4.2 Annual Renewal

All remote work arrangements must be renewed annually during the performance review cycle. Failure to renew results in automatic conversion to on-site status.
